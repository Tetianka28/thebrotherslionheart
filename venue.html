Info for wheelchair users

There is wheelchair access to all halls, there are several wheelchair-friendly toilets in the building, and wheelchair users have the option of bringing a companion with them for free. When purchasing a ticket, remember to state that you need a wheelchair space when you book, and kindly indicate if you wish to have a companion with you. Leaders must have an approved companion card.

Access with companion

If you are a wheelchair user, you must use your wheelchair during the event. Your companion will be provided with a seat free of charge, usually next to you. There is a limited number of companion tickets per performance, so it is first come, first served.

Parking

The Esbjerg Concert Hall does not have its own parking lot, but fortunately there are good opportunities to park elsewhere near the Concert Hall.
